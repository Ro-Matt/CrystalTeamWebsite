Competetion

Competition Matrix

"Weak" Competition

clickWrapped - Only provides a simplified version of the ToS/ToC of the websites and services on their website.
 TLDRLegal - Provides a little more in comparison to clickWrapped. They provide a user account to save what website and services each user has. A simplified version of the ToS/ToC contracts of the websites and services in their database. They also allow their community to post their findings and explanations of the ToS/ToC in question.

Biggest Competition

Terms of Service; Didn't Read - Has the most robust website of the three. ToS;DR allows community members to add their findings, a user account for its community members, and a privacy ranking.

Why We Will Be Better

There are a few things that the competition does that we would like to apply to our website as well

  • Easy explanations of ToS/ToC contracts in plain English for user's to read and understand. 
  • A single email User Account so that our users will be able to keep tabs of every service and website is connected to their email.
  • Community Involvement will be a necessity, this will allow our bot to become more robust faster than if it was left to only admins.
  • We would also add a privacy rating to every service/website being added to our website.

These are things we believe are the bare minimum for any product that is trying to help people in the real world understand the legal jargon of any ToS/ToC.

The folloinwg list will be what separates us from the competition.

  • Privacy visualization made of graphs and charts for our users that are visual learners
  • Automatic scraping for our bot when an admin or a user submits a new website or service's ToS/ToC
    • This will allow for a more instantaneous explanation for our users. The more ToS/ToC our bot is exposed to the faster it will be able to help any user.
  • ToS/ToC comparisons, for similar services such as but not limited to:
    • Netflix Vs. Hulu
    • OneDrive vs. Dropbox vs. GoogleDrive
    • YouTube vs. Twitch
  • Finally, have user activity monitoring where we will keep the user's account up to date with any updates to the ToS/ToC connected to the account.
