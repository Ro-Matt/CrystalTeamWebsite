Risks

Customer Risks

Risks:
C1: User needs to view the most up to date user agreements
C2: Making decisions based off of inaccurate translation of user agreement

Mitigations:
C1: User can visit the company’s website to view the most recent TOS
C2: Users can decide to get professional legal help



Technical Risks

Risks:
T1: Storing other companies’ user agreement may not be allowed 
T2: An update to a user agreement would require immediate update to annotations 
T3:  Each annotation of a user agreement would need to be accurate

Mitigations:
T1: State where the TOS can be found and which section each annotation refers to
T2: Update annotations and update the user that changes have been made
T3: We can get help from a professional to create annotations

Security Risks

Risks:
S1: Our database containing our annotations, companies’ TOS, and other sensitive data may be breached (SQL injection)
S2: An adversary may gain unauthorized access to our users’ accounts

Mitigations:
S1: Implement techniques such as parameterized statements 
S2: Use most effective encryption techniques

Legal Risks

Risks:
L1: Customer may use our translation of TOS to make a decision and try to hold us accountable if anything goes wrong
L2: Companies may sue because we are taking their TOS and storing it in our database

Mitigations:
L1: Clearly state that our translation of TOS is not to serve as legal advice
L2: Reference the TOS and state where the user can find the TOS and the section the annotations refer to
